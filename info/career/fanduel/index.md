# FanDuel

My tenure at [FanDuel](https://www.fanduel.com/) from August 2016 to December 2019 shaped my professional foundation more than any other role. Three and a half years of daily operational reporting, complex financial modeling, and the kind of team environment I've been chasing ever since.

I joined straight out of Bucknell as a business analyst and left as a senior analyst with deep expertise in fantasy sports economics, data engineering, and building tools that non-technical stakeholders actually want to use.
